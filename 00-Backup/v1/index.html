ESG Fusion offers valuable comprehensive analysis that can be applied in a number of business critical areas

  1. Best-in-class and best-in-universe investment analysis

    Comparable company scores support flexible applications for best-in-class analysis, including industry/sector or regional approaches; ERM’s sector risk approach also facilitates comparisons across sectors

    Multi-dimensional ESG risk scores can be incorporated into equity bond valuation models and aggregated at the portfolio level.

    ESG risk scores can be incorporated into a long-term investment strategy featuring equities that will benefit from themes relating to environmental, social or governance factors.
